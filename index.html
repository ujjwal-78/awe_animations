About Awe Animations

Who we are | Why we do what we do

We’re an animated video content creation company that makes video content for every stage of your sales process and corporate communications. We’re a compact team of creative creatives and we love what we do.

Our operations are based in New Dehli, India (Delhi NCR) and Banglore (Karnataka). Over the years we’ve been crafting captivating videos that tell inspiring stories about brands, businesses, and many other things. Give us a call or send a quick note — we’d love to hear from you.

Our Services

Explainer Videos

Explain your business, product, or idea with animated explainer videos. This type of video animation is your elevator pitch — the why-we-matter short and sweet message. We’ll simplify your message to the level your audience can understand. Explainer videos are effective in showcasing your products, what problem you solve, and how you solve your customer’s problem.

Video Animation

We make custom animated videos that helps in advertise your business, educate your audience or tell your story. We’ll set realistic goals and develop a winning video marketing strategy that incorporate well-crafted animation videos. Animated videos use graphics and characters in a motion sequence designed to illustrate a concept or tell a story.

Startup Videos

Our Startup video production services are designed to help Startups explain what they do and clearly delivery their value proposition. Use video as a platform for introducing your product to investors for funding, or to launch your product and also help investors understand the problem you solve, and customers move closer to a buying decision in 1-2 minutes.

Coprorate Videos

Corporate videos are an essential component of online marketing. Create a video that drives a positive image, humanizes your brand, and builds trust. We create video content for every stage of your sales funnel and corporate communications. More businesses are leveraging the power of video to create compelling content that drives traffic and boosts business growth.

Branded Videos

Make branded videos that drive a desired message whilst keeping your brand in the background. Content marketing is an essential component of every marketing strategy. We can create sponsored non-sales videos that can potentially drive traffic to your business and improve brand visibility & let audience connect emotionally while keeping your brand in the background.

Whiteboard Video

We understand that whiteboard videos are great at delivering compelling content. Using tried and tested methods we develop a sales strategy for your video, write the script, create visuals and animation that will deliver tangible results and also convey your message with simplicity and clarity. We’re ready to partner with your organization and champion your cause.

Motion Graphics
